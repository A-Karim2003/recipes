Plov

Plov is a Central Asian rice dish made with meat, vegetables, and spices, all cooked together for rich, aromatic flavor. Popular in Uzbekistan, it features ingredients like lamb, carrots, onions, and cumin. Variations exist with different meats and spices.

Beef spaghetti

Beef spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti noodles mixed with a savory beef sauce. The sauce is made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and Italian herbs like basil and oregano. It’s often topped with Parmesan cheese and served hot.

lasagne

Lasagne is an Italian pasta dish made with layers of lasagna noodles, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese. The meat sauce typically includes ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic, seasoned with Italian herbs. It's baked until bubbly and golden, often topped with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
